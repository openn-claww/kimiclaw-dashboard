# PolyClaw Trading Rules

## Risk Management
- **Max Risk Per Trade:** 8% of portfolio
- **Dry Run Mode:** Enabled (simulated trades, no real execution)
- **Heartbeat Interval:** 15 minutes

## Trading Strategy
1. Monitor trending markets for opportunities
2. Scan for hedge opportunities (Tier 1-2 only)
3. Check positions and P&L regularly
4. Alert on significant market movements

## Safety Rules
- Never exceed 8% risk per position
- Prefer hedged positions over naked exposure
- Monitor gas costs on Polygon
- Keep POL balance above 5 for gas fees

## Commands to Run Every 15min (Dry Run)
- Check wallet status
- Scan trending markets
- Check existing positions
- Scan for hedge opportunities
- Report findings
