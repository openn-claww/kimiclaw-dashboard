# HOW TO CREATE DISCORD WEBHOOK URL (Step-by-Step)

## STEP 1: JOIN THE SERVER
1. Click the invite link (e.g., https://discord.gg/polymarket)
2. Accept the invite
3. You're now in the server

---

## STEP 2: FIND THE CHANNEL TO MONITOR
1. Look at the channel list on the left
2. Find channels like:
   - #alpha
   - #signals
   - #announcements
   - #trading
   - #general

---

## STEP 3: CREATE WEBHOOK

### Method A: Right-Click (Desktop/Browser)
1. **Right-click** on the channel name
2. Click **"Edit Channel"**
3. Click **"Integrations"** in left sidebar
4. Click **"Create Webhook"**
5. Give it a name (e.g., "My Bot")
6. Click **"Copy Webhook URL"**
7. Done! Send me the URL

### Method B: Channel Settings (Mobile/Desktop)
1. Click the **channel name** at top
2. Click the **gear icon** (Settings)
3. Click **"Integrations"**
4. Click **"Webhooks"**
5. Click **"New Webhook"**
6. Name it "Trading Bot" or anything
7. Click **"Copy Webhook URL"**
8. Done! Send me the URL

---
