## Top Strategies Identified (with Confidence Scores)

### 1. Information Arbitrage / "Neighbor Effect" Strategy
**Confidence: 95%** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Concept:** Exploit information asymmetries by asking better questions than the crowd.

**Case Study:** French trader Théo (Fredi9999) made $85 million on the 2024 U.S. election by commissioning YouGov polls asking "Who do you think your neighbors will vote for?" rather than direct voting intentions. This revealed hidden Trump support that mainstream polls missed.

**Execution:**
- Commission targeted polls in swing states/demographics
- Use local language sources, Telegram channels for early signals
- Track "cultural calendar alpha" - governments avoid embarrassing news near holidays

**Risk Level:** Medium (requires capital and research infrastructure)

### 2. Cross-Platform Arbitrage (Polymarket ↔ Kalshi ↔ Sportsbooks)
**Confidence: 90%** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Concept:** Exploit price differences for identical events across platforms.

**Academic Backing:** IMDEA Networks Institute study analyzed 86M+ transactions and found **$40M+ in arbitrage profits** extracted between April 2024-April 2025.

**Types:**
- **Same-Market:** When YES + NO prices don't sum to $1.00 (0.5-2% returns, closes in ~200ms)
- **Cross-Platform:** Buy low on one platform, sell high on another
- **Combinatorial:** Exploit logical inconsistencies between related markets

**Example:**
- Polymarket: Trump wins at 62% ($0.62)
- Kalshi: Trump wins at 68% ($0.68)
- Profit: $0.06/share (9.7% return)

**Risk Level:** Low (near-guaranteed if executed correctly)

**Note:** Requires automated bots for execution - manual trading too slow.

### 7. Correlation Trading
**Confidence: 70%** ⭐⭐⭐

**Concept:** Identify markets that should move together but are temporarily mispriced.

**Example:**
- "Trump wins presidency" at 45%
- "Republicans control Senate" at 35%
- Historical correlation: When Trump wins, Republicans win Senate ~85% of the time
- Expected Senate odds: 45% × 85% = 38%
- Trade: Buy Senate at 35% (underpriced)

**Risk Level:** Medium (correlations can break)

### 10. Portfolio Management & Kelly Criterion
**Confidence: 90%** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Concept:** Profitability comes from surviving long enough for edges to compound.

**Kelly Formula:** Optimal bet size = (Edge × Odds) / (Odds - 1)

**Example:**
- You estimate 60% probability
- Market price: $0.45
- Edge: 0.15
- Full Kelly: 27% bankroll
- **Pro approach:** Half-Kelly = 13.5% max per trade

**Portfolio Construction ($10,000 example):**
- 50% core positions (5-7 high-conviction trades)
- 30% tactical trades (10-15 medium conviction)
- 20% cash reserve

**Rules:**
- No more than 3 positions in one category
- Mix time horizons
- Max 40-50% in single position (avoid gambling)
